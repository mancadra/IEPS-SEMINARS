**Banana Torte Recipe**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed  
- 1/2 cup butter (melted)  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 2 eggs  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1 1/2 cups flour  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- 1/2 cup milk (optional, for moisture)  

**Steps**:  
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round cake pan.  
2. In a bowl, mix mashed bananas, melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.  
3. Gradually add flour and baking powder, stirring until smooth. Add milk if desired.  
4. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 40–45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.  
5. Let cool, then slice into layers. For a torte, assemble layers with custard or whipped cream (e.g., banana custard made with eggs, sugar, and cream). Top with chocolate shavings or nuts.  

**Note**: For a custard filling, mix 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup flour, 2 eggs, 1 cup cream, and 1/2 cup banana puree, then cook until thickened.

**Bavarian Cream Recipe**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 4 egg yolks  
- 1/2 cup sugar  
- 1 cup heavy cream  
- 1 tsp vanilla extract  
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickness)  

**Instructions**:  
1. In a saucepan, whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch until smooth.  
2. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and reaches 170°F (79°C) (custard stage).  
3. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.  
4. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.  
5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cooled custard until fully combined.  
6. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or until firm.  
7. Serve in glasses or cups, optionally topped with caramelized sugar or chocolate sauce.  

**Note**: For a chocolate version, substitute 1/4 cup melted chocolate for 1/4 cup of the cream.

Question: Kako se pripravi jabolčni kompot?

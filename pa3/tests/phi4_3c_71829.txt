**Bavarian Cream Recipe**

*Ingredients:*
- 2 cups milk
- 6 large egg yolks
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons unflavored gelatin
- 1/4 cup water (for blooming gelatin)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or melted and cooled chocolate
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

*Instructions:*
1. **Prepare the Gelatin**: Sprinkle gelatin over cold water in a small bowl to bloom for about 5 minutes.
2. **Make the Custard Base**: Heat milk until just simmering. In another bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick. Gradually add hot milk to yolk mixture while stirring constantly. Return to saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring continuously until it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon.
3. **Incorporate Gelatin**: Remove from heat and stir in bloomed gelatin until dissolved. Add vanilla extract or melted chocolate if using.
4. **Cool the Mixture**: Pour custard into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap directly on the surface, and chill for about 30 minutes.
5. **Whip the Cream**: Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form.
6. **Combine**: Gently fold whipped cream into chilled custard mixture until smooth.
7. **Set**: Pour Bavarian cream into serving dishes or molds and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

### Chocolate Truffle Recipe

#### Ingredients
- 200g (7 oz) dark chocolate, finely chopped
- 150ml (5 fl oz) heavy cream
- Cocoa powder for dusting (or other toppings like nuts or sprinkles)

#### Instructions
1. **Melt the Chocolate**: Place the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
2. **Heat the Cream**: Bring the heavy cream to just below boiling point in a small saucepan.
3. **Combine**: Pour the hot cream over the chocolate, let sit for 1-2 minutes, then stir until smooth and glossy.
4. **Chill**: Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm.
5. **Shape Truffles**: Scoop portions of the mixture with a teaspoon or melon baller, roll into balls using your hands.
6. **Coat**: Roll each truffle in cocoa powder or desired coating.
7. **Chill Again**: Refrigerate on a parchment-lined baking sheet for 30 minutes until set.

#### Storage
- Keep in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

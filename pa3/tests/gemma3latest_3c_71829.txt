Here's a basic recipe for Bavarian Cream:

**Ingredients:**

*   2 cups (480 ml) Heavy Cream
*   1/2 cup (100g) Granulated Sugar
*   1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
*   1/4 teaspoon Salt
*   1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon Gelatin Powder (Bloom in 2 tablespoons cold water for 5 minutes) *or* 1/2 - 1 teaspoon Agar-Agar powder

**Instructions:**

1.  **Bloom Gelatin (if using):**  If using gelatin, bloom it in cold water for 5-10 minutes until softened.
2.  **Whip Cream:** In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream with an electric mixer until soft peaks form.
3.  **Add Sugar & Vanilla:** Gradually add the sugar and vanilla extract while continuing to whip until stiff peaks form.
4.  **Add Gelatin (if using):**  If using gelatin, add the bloomed gelatin to the whipped cream and whip for another 30 seconds until fully incorporated. If using agar-agar, whisk it in until dissolved.
5.  **Chill:** Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the cream to set completely.

**Notes:**

*   For a smoother texture, strain the Bavarian cream through a fine-mesh sieve before serving.
*   Adjust the sugar to your preference.
